When she first moved in, she had a hard time getting used to a new place

We learned she was angry most of the time, and didn't like many things, such as people..

Especially me. She hated me.

She liked a few things, like her toys, her bed and food.

But all together she just liked being left alone. we came to accept that Cece just wasnt a very social or cuddly cat.

But then, one random Afternoon in November,

Mum came home with a bag of Christmas decorations.

she became inseparable from this shiny mass of plastic She would stare at it for hours, and walk with it around her head.

She would use it to keep warm while watching the birds outside, and we definetly wern't allowed to move it..

Wherever she was, the tinsel would be too, either acting as a bed, a toy or a friend.

All winter long shes obsessed with it

Now every year, as soon as it's in the shops, we buy her a new piece. So she can make new nests and new circles and feel at home once again.
